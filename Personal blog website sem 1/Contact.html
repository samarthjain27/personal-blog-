    Also i was not having any friends here in Bangalore . I was all alone before the college started .
    As days passed I became familiar with the city and I was feeling confident .Bangalore is known as the "Silicon Valley of India"
    The climate is one of the best aspects of Bangalore. Since it is at an elevation of 920m the weather is pleasant throughout the year, even during the summer months.
    Bangalore has a very competitive cost of living in comparison to other metro cities in India. As a student it's a little tough for me because everything here in bangalore is very costly .
    Being a student in Bangalore you have to deal with so many things like hostel / Pg rent , travel expenses , and also food is a major problem in the hostels and pg’s .
