    • Trip To Goa

    My trip to goa was full of enjoyment and adventures . It was a five day trip from 22nd November to 27th november . On 22nd morning me and my family reached Goa and got welcomed by the cool breeze . After reaching the goa airport we went to our selected beachside resort .
    In the afternoon we spent time at our resort relaxing by the pool . In the evening we headed towards the nearby beach shack to enjoy some local goan cuisines . don't forget to try dishes like poi bread , goan bun , and also their famous kaju feni . When the first batch of the season comes it's called orrak which is a very famous cocktail of goa .
    Then we went to anjuna flea market to explore Goa's local items and from there we bought many things like clothes , accessories , and local food items. After that we were very tired so we went to a local restaurant and had food .

    At night we went to the beach side clubs . Some of the famous beach side clubs in north Goa are titos lane , club cubana , and mambos are popular options for dancing and socialising . After that we returned to our beach side resort and after the day full of fun and adventures we slept to enjoy our another day in goa .

    On the second day of our trip we started our cheerful morning with a hearty breakfast at our accommodation .
    Then we headed towards north Goa which is known for its lively beaches .
    We visited Baga beach , calangute beach , and anjuna beach for sunbathing , water sports , and beachside activities . We can rent the water sports equipment or we can take a banana ride .

    We also visited the famous dudh sagar waterfalls which is approx 80 kms from Goa . Dudhsagar Waterfall is located in the high peaks of the Western Ghats and it is a sight specially to visit in monsoons when it is in full flow . From a distance the waterfall appears like milk rushing down the mountains .


    In the evening we visited the to the historic Aguada fort and after visiting the aguada fort we took a sunset cruise along the mandovi river . We also visited the famous “ALL ABOUT ALCOHOL ” store in goa which provides various informations about alcohol and its glasses and at last of the tour they gave us their own invented 5 cocktails to try .

    It was a very informative and a nice experience visiting to the “ALL ABOUT ALCOHOL ” store . Now it's our third day in Goa , After the breakfast we were all set to explore the south Goa for a more relaxed atmosphere . After reaching south Goa we visited the palolem beach known for its beauty and calm waters . Then we took a kayaking experience . After the fabulous kayaking experience we took a boat trip to spot dolphins .

    After that we went to various points in south goa for exploring and enjoying the vibe of goa . Goa is a place which everyone should at least visit once in their lifetime.
    After exploring the points we went back to our resort and spent our free time playing beach games with the family.
    Also we visited the shri mangeshi temple to experience some of Goa's cultural and natural beauty . The Mangeshi temple or the Manguesh Devasthan is perhaps the most famous of all Goan temples.
    It is located at Priol in Ponda taluka, about 21 kilometers from the capital city of Panaji. The surrounding area is known as Mangueshi. In ancient times the temple was located at Kushasthali The Shri Mangeshi Temple is a beautiful temple dedicated to Lord Shiva. It is considered to be the most important temple in Goa
    On the fourth day after the breakfast we went for a half day adventure tour on the trek through the western ghats and also we took an ATV ride experience through the jungle .
    After the half day trek we went for taking a sunset cruise along the river with cultural performances and a goan buffet dinner .

    Then we checked out our accommodation and stored our laguage . Then we visited old goa where we explored historical and cultural attractions such as the basilica of bom jesus and st . augustin tower .
    Then we spent our afternoon shopping at the markets in panaji for clothing and local handicrafts . After the shopping scenes we enjoyed a traditional goan lunch at a local restaurant . Then we headed towards the airport for our flight .
    Overall The trip to goa was a fantastic and an adventures experience and i recommend that everyone should visit goa once in their life time .
    This five day trip to Goa was a mix of relaxation , adventure , culture , and natural beauty which made it an unforgettable experience for me and my family .
